# Obesity and Diabetes in Latin America (2000-2024)

[![Mendeley Data](https://img.shields.io/badge/Mendeley%20Data-k8ksc5zxyr-red)](https://data.mendeley.com/drafts/k8ksc5zxyr) [![License: CC BY 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC%20BY%204.0-lightgrey.svg)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) [![Files](https://img.shields.io/badge/Files-21-blue)]()

## Description
Epidemiological, clinical and health-economic data on obesity and type 2 diabetes in Latin America (2000-2024). Covers 18 countries with prevalence rates, mortality statistics, healthcare costs, insulin access, metabolic risk factors and national intervention programs.

**Mendeley Data:** [k8ksc5zxyr](https://data.mendeley.com/drafts/k8ksc5zxyr) | **Spanish version:** [obesidad-diabetes-america-latina](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/obesidad-diabetes-america-latina)

## Key Statistics (2024)
| Indicator | Value | Source |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Regional diabetes prevalence | 10.8% | IDF Atlas 2023 |
| Highest prevalence country | Mexico (15.2%) | IDF Atlas 2023 |
| Adults with diabetes in LATAM | 34.2 million | IDF Atlas 2023 |
| Undiagnosed cases | ~40% | IDF Atlas 2023 |
| Annual healthcare cost | USD 59.4 billion | IDF/PAHO |
| Annual attributable deaths | ~290,000 | PAHO ENLACE |

## Files
- analysis_notebook.ipynb — Jupyter analysis notebook
- obesity_diabetes_prevalence_latam.csv — Prevalence by country/year
- diabetes_mortality_latam.csv — Mortality by country/year
- healthcare_costs_by_country.csv — Direct costs in USD
- idf_atlas_summary_en.csv — IDF Atlas summary 2009-2023
- obesity_bmi_trends_en.csv — Adult BMI trends
- paho_diabetes_indicators_en.csv — PAHO indicators
- metabolic_risk_factors_en.csv — Hypertension, cholesterol etc.
- diabetes_by_age_gender_en.csv — Age/gender breakdown
- obesity_children_adolescents_en.csv — Children and adolescents
- national_diabetes_programs_en.csv — National programs
- food_environment_latam_en.csv — UPF, SSB, food availability
- insulin_access_affordability_en.csv — Insulin access and prices
- diabetes_complications_en.csv — Nephropathy, retinopathy etc.
- gdp_health_expenditure_comparison_en.csv — GDP and health spending
- regional_obesity_policies_en.md — National policy summary
- literature_review_obesity_diabetes_en.bib — Bibliography
- methodology_notes_en.md — Methodology and sources
- data_dictionary_en.md — Variable descriptions
